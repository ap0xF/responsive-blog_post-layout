Food group What it does? Source
Protien They help your body grow and repair themselves. You find them in meats, dairy products, poultry, eggs and beans.
CARBOHYDRATE They give you energy You find them in sugary and starchy foods like potatoes, rice, cereals, pasta, bread and some fruits and vegetables.
FATS They provide energy and help in building cell membranes You find them in dairy products, red meats some poultry and fish.
MINERALS Iron is good for the blood. Calcium is s good for your bones. Magnesium is good for your nerves They are found especially in fresh fruits and vegetables
VITAMINS Vitamins are good for keeping your blood healthy. They are good for skin bones and teath Vitamins are mostly found in dairy products (milk, eggs, butter), fresh fruit and vegetables

FIBRE It helps you digests your food You find fibre in lots of food like cereals, fruit, bread and vegetables.
